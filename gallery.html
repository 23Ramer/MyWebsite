Introduction

This is a complation of what I believe to be some of the most inovative, impactful, infamous, and all around best games I have ever played. Below there is also a semi-brief discription of what the game is about.

Carcassonne

Carcassonne is a game where you have to get the most points by claiming roads, monesatries, and castles. But the only why to claim them is place down differnt tiles that contain the territory you are tring to claim. After all the tiles have been placed down, you add up all the points and who ever has the most points wins. Their are a variety of expantions that contain more pieces that provide differnt advatanges and challenges.

Catan

The Settlers of Catan is a game where differnt playes compete to get a set amount of victory points before the other players. After the hexagonal board has been set up, players place down where they want their first settlements and cities to be. Then players take turns roling the dice and collecting resources they can use to build various items including settlements, cities, roads, and development cards they can use for their advatange in the game.

Monopoly

Monopoly is a game that allows players to learn who is smart and who is poor. Players take turns rolling the dice to move their game token across the board. While traveling around the board players have to buy propities and collect a set of propities. Once they do that they will be able to upgade the propities they have and make money off them. If a player lands on a proprity they don't own, they have to pay the player who owns it money. You win if everyone else becomes bunkrupt and can't pay any one else.

Srcabble

Scrabble is a game where players have to use little individual pieces that have letters on them to spell out a word on a premade board. You get points for the number of letters you place on the board, as well as the difficulty and complexity of the word. On the board, there are certian bonuses you can get if you place your pieces in the right spot. When no more words can be created, the player with the most points wins. It is also sugested that people always have a dictionary near by in case of arguments.

Ticket to Ride

Ticket to ride is a game where players get points by creating train routes to connect different destinations. Players pick up cards that contian train track that can only be use for certain train routes. There are only a limited amount of routes that can be created, so players have train stations that only allowed to hijack one other opponent's train routes. Whenever someone has 0-2 trains left, every player goes again one more time and whoever has the most points by then, wins.

Chess

Chess is one of the ultimate games of stratigy that exists. 2 players face off each other with idential armies and fight each other untill one side can capture the other's king. The key to winning chess is to know what your opponent is going to do next. Once you figure that out, you have to play your pieces in a way that would allow you to bring the game to a quicker end. Even if it means sacrificing your pieces to do so, the goal is to take your opponent's king by any means necessary.
