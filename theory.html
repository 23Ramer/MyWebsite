Mathmatics

No matter what game you play, there is always a way to use mathimatical probobliity to your advantage. Whether if it's the role of a die or what card you will draw next, there is always a way to predict the outcome resonably.

Timing

One of the lesser known and more risky stratigies of playing boardgames, timing is the decision to not immediately play what looks like to be a good opportunity right now, but to save and consolidate your resources for an even bigger play.

Get to know the people you will be playing against. Get to know their tells, their signs of weaknesses and most importantly their signs of strength. When you know a person in the game you are playing is going to make a big move soon, you need to either make sure you can stop it before it starts, get other people to stop it, or move out of the way of the player's blast radius so that you do not get severely damaged by their play.

Catan

The Settlers of Catan is a game where differnt playes compete to get a set amount of victory points before the other players. After the hexagonal board has been set up, players place down where they want their first settlements and cities to be. Then players take turns roling the dice and collecting resources they can use to build various items including settlements, cities, roads, and development cards they can use for their advatange in the game.

Monopoly

Monopoly is a game that allows players to learn who is smart and who is poor. Players take turns rolling the dice to move their game token across the board. While traveling around the board players have to buy propities and collect a set of propities. Once they do that they will be able to upgade the propities they have and make money off them. If a player lands on a proprity they don't own, they have to pay the player who owns it money. You win if everyone else becomes bunkrupt and can't pay any one else.
